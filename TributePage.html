Nikola Tesla

The man who powered nations

Tesla's Patents and Inventions

  • 1882 - Discovers the rotating magnetic field
  • 1887 - Registers patent for electro-magnetic motors
  • 1891 - Invents a transformer for the production of high voltage and frequency electricity

"There's not a lot of modern conveniences that we currently enjoy that weren't touched by Nikola Tesla in some way,"

-- Marc Alessi, executive director of the Tesla Science Center at Wardenclyffe in New York
